**Query**: Orders completed hourly:
Analyze and present the distribution of completed orders on an hourly basis.

**Solution**:
```sql
SELECT
	HOUR(status_datetime) AS hours,
	COUNT(*)
FROM
	order_status
WHERE
	order_status.status_id = 'ORDER_COMPLETED'
GROUP BY
	hours;
```
